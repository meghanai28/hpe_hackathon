Crash break for CPSLO-108: db_load also trusts file_count and allocates
file_count * sizeof(FileMeta) plus file_count pointers with no bound checks.

On the 32-bit contest build, file_count 1073741824 wraps those allocation sizes
to zero. The loader then reads a FileMeta into the undersized allocation and/or
walks a huge corrupted file_data array during cleanup. The malformed fresh
database should segfault, abort, or timeout.
